Backpack helps you to put all your different content into one central Content Management System.

Here's just a few things you can do:

Integrated WYSIWYG Editor for HTML documents
Integrated code editor for various formats (XML, JSON, etc.)
Upload images
Edit list and menus
Define and validate additional form data
